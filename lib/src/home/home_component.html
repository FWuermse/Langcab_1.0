Langcab is a simple webapp designed for capturing your vocabulary.

When learning a language it's not always easy to remember all the vocabulary. Langcab will help you remember your words by using a spaced repetition algorithm.
Even the process of adding words into the system will steady your vocabulary. You can add as many languages as you want.
To use it just sign in and start by adding your first word!

Why use Langcab

Spaced repetition

Langcab will automatically detect how fast you learn and lets you recall every word often enough so you don't forget it, but just as often as necessary to remember. This is currently the most effective way known, to study vocabulary.

Bring your vocabulary everywhere

Langcab saves your words online and allows you to access your words everywhere you have an internet connection. No matter if you're on your phone, browser or desktop. As long as you log in, you will see your words.

Find your words

When learning a language it often happens that we forget a certain word but when we try to look it up using a translator it will show more than ten possible translations and we don't know which one it was we've learned. With Langcab you can create your own dictionary and find the words you've learned immediately.

From learners for learners

Developed by people who are passionate about learning languages. Any feedback is more than welcome. If you have an idea on how to improve this website please go ahead and send us a message via email.

Multiple languages

If you are learning multiple languages at a time, Langcab is giving you the option of adding as many languages as you want. You are also not limited by the original version of a word. You could learn from Spanish to English or from German to Chinese. Up to you!email.

The best way to use Langcab

Learn by writing

Many studies have found out that writing a word helps us remember it. Especially for languages with a different alphabet. It is highly efficient to physically write down words when adding as well as studying them. Together with the spaced repetition algorithm studying will be much easier. To avoid wasting paper it may be worth buying a small cleanable board.

Constantly add words

Try to set yourself an amount of words per week that you want to learn. It's important to constantly add words to your dictionary to avoid plateaus. If you constantly increase your active vocabulary you can make sure that you are still improving. The app will take care of making sure you remember the words entered in the past.
